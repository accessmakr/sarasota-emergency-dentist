Your Free Sedation Dentistry Checklist

McKinsey-level guide: Preparation, what to expect, and aftercare for nitrous, oral & IV sedation in Sarasota.

1. Discuss full medical history & medications with provider✅ 48 hours prior
2. Fasting instructions (no food/drink 6–8 hrs for oral/IV)✅ Critical for safety
3. Arrange a responsible driver – you cannot drive after sedation✅ Mandatory
4. Wear comfortable clothing (loose sleeves for IV access)✅ Practical tip
5. Bring a trusted companion if possible✅ Emotional support
6. No alcohol or recreational substances 24 hrs prior✅ Safety first
7. Post-sedation: Rest 4–6 hrs, no heavy machinery✅ Recovery protocol
8. Pediatric patients: Special calming techniques & parent presence✅ Child-focused
9. Know your sedation level (mild/moderate/deep) before appointment✅ Informed consent
10. Follow-up call from provider within 24 hrs post-procedure✅ Sarasota standard
