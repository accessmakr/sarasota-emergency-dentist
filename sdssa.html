Your free oral appliance checklist

Instant PDF guide: Everything you need to know about sleep apnea oral appliances in Sarasota — preparation, fitting, aftercare, and insurance tips.

1. Get a sleep study first (home or lab)✅ Required for diagnosis
2. Bring your sleep study results to appointment✅ Most providers accept recent studies
3. Custom impressions or digital scan (15–30 min)✅ Same-day or next-day fitting possible
4. Appliance delivery & adjustment (usually 1–2 visits)✅ Wear nightly – portable & quiet
5. Follow-up titration (2–4 weeks)✅ Fine-tune for best AHI reduction
6. Daily cleaning: warm water + mild soap✅ Brush appliance like teeth
7. Expected results: 50–80% snoring reduction✅ Many achieve CPAP-level results
8. Travel tip: Appliance fits in small case✅ TSA-friendly
9. Insurance: Many plans cover OAT (verify codes)✅ Ask provider for pre-authorization
10. Annual check-up: Appliance durability ~3–5 years✅ Monitor fit & bite
