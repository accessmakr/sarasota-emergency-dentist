About Sarasota Emergency Dentist

Last updated: April 7, 2026

We are an independent, locally focused directory service founded in 2024 to solve one problem: when a Sarasota resident has a dental emergency, they should be able to find a verified, high-quality provider in seconds — not hours.

Our Mission

To connect Sarasota County residents with the 21 best emergency-capable dental practices through transparent, real-time, clinically verified information.

How We Curate the 21 Providers

  • Every provider is physically located inside Sarasota County
  • Minimum 4.5-star average rating across Google, Yelp, and Healthgrades (50+ reviews)
  • Documented same-day or after-hours emergency capability
  • Active Florida Board of Dentistry license verified weekly
  • ADA emergency protocol compliance

We are not affiliated with any dental chain or insurance company. Our only goal is faster pain relief for Sarasota families.
