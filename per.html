Your free periodontics care checklist

Instant PDF guide: What to do for gum disease, recession, bleeding gums & post-surgical care — plus local periodontist map.

1. Bleeding gums when brushing or flossing✅ Gentle warm salt rinse • Book scaling exam
2. Gum recession exposing tooth roots✅ Avoid aggressive brushing • See specialist for graft evaluation
3. Persistent bad breath or metallic taste✅ Could indicate infection • Immediate periodontal evaluation
4. Loose teeth or shifting bite✅ Urgent assessment to save bone • Same-day pocket measurement
5. Deep gum pockets or swelling✅ Scaling & root planing or surgery may be needed
6. Painful gums or abscess near tooth✅ Do not pop • Seek immediate drainage & antibiotics
7. History of smoking, diabetes or heart disease✅ Higher risk – schedule annual perio screening
8. Post-graft or surgery care✅ Ice 10 min on/off • Soft diet • No smoking • Follow instructions
