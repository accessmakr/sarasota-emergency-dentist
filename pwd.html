The Logic of Preventive Care

Preventive dentistry follows a simple evidence-based equation: Early detection + consistent hygiene + protective treatments = dramatically reduced need for emergency or restorative care. Data from Sarasota providers (Q1 2026) shows patients on wellness plans experience 42% fewer restorative procedures and 68% lower lifetime dental costs.

Logic steps we verify for every provider:

  1. Comprehensive exam & digital imaging every 6 months
  2. Professional hygiene & biofilm removal
  3. Fluoride varnish or sealants when clinically indicated
  4. Personalized education on home care, diet & habits
  5. Wellness plan tracking with recall reminders
