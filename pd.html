Your free pediatric emergency dental checklist

Instant PDF guide for parents: What to do for any child dental emergency + local pediatric provider map.

1. Knocked-out tooth (permanent or baby)✅ Milk or saliva • 60 min window
2. Severe toothache or swelling✅ Warm salt rinse • Call pediatric specialist
3. Abscess with fever✅ Do not pop • Immediate care needed
4. Broken or chipped tooth✅ Save fragments • Gentle rinse
5. Child refuses dentist (anxiety)✅ Nitrous or sedation options available
6. Object stuck in tooth/gum✅ Floss gently • Professional removal
7. Loose baby tooth trauma✅ Monitor • Pediatric evaluation same-day
8. Facial trauma or jaw injury✅ Ice • ER if breathing issue
