Your Free Implant Dentistry Checklist

Instant PDF guide: What to do for implant emergencies, post-op care, All-on-4® recovery & signs of failure — plus local specialist map.

1. Loose or wobbly implant✅ Do not chew on it • Call specialist immediately • Same-day rescue available
2. Pain or swelling around implant site✅ Warm salt rinses • Avoid heat • Urgent evaluation needed
3. Failed or rejected implant✅ Do not attempt removal yourself • Specialist removal & replacement same-day possible
4. All-on-4® bridge feels unstable✅ Avoid hard foods • Contact provider within 24 hours
5. Post-surgical bleeding after implant placement✅ Bite on gauze 30–45 min • Call if persists
6. Bone graft site pain or exposure✅ Ice 10 min on/off • No straws or smoking • Immediate follow-up
7. Sinus lift complications (pressure/pain)✅ No nose blowing • Antibiotics may be prescribed • Call surgeon
8. Implant crown or abutment came off✅ Save pieces • Temporary cement or sugar-free gum • Same-day repair
9. Infection signs (pus, fever, bad taste)✅ Do not ignore • Same-day incision & drainage at implant specialist
10. Difficulty chewing or bite feels off✅ Schedule adjustment • May indicate integration issue
11. Allergic reaction to implant materials✅ Stop any medication • Seek emergency care if swelling affects airway
12. Post-implant diet & care (first 2 weeks)✅ Soft foods only • No smoking • Gentle brushing
13. Long-term maintenance for implants✅ Professional cleanings every 3–4 months • Annual x-rays
14. Insurance / financing for implants✅ Many providers offer payment plans • Check individual listings
