Our Methodology & Logic for Special Needs Provider Selection

Providers are selected only after triple verification: (1) Florida Board of Dentistry active license with no disciplinary actions, (2) documented experience treating autism, sensory disorders, cerebral palsy, Down syndrome or medically complex patients via patient reviews and self-reported protocols, (3) ADA Special Care Dentistry alignment. We cross-reference Google, Yelp, Healthgrades, and direct outreach. Logic prioritizes patient outcomes: zero-wait sensory accommodations, sedation success rates >90%, and family-reported first-visit completion rates.
