Your Free Geriatric Dentistry Checklist

McKinsey-level guide: Senior-specific preparation, medication safety, denture care & in-home tips for Sarasota seniors 65+.

1. Bring full medication list & physician contacts to appointment✅ Critical for safety
2. Discuss dry mouth (xerostomia) from common senior meds✅ Common issue
3. Ask about Medicare coverage or senior payment plans✅ Financial clarity
4. Caregiver should attend if possible (especially for dementia)✅ Supportive care
5. Inquire about in-home/mobile options if homebound✅ Geriatric House Call
6. Bone density & osteoporosis impact on implants/dentures✅ Discuss with provider
7. Denture cleaning routine & nighttime removal✅ Daily habit
8. Schedule same-day adjustments for new dentures✅ Comfort first
9. Post-procedure: Soft foods & hydration for seniors✅ Recovery tips
10. Follow-up within 48 hours for any swelling or discomfort✅ Sarasota standard
