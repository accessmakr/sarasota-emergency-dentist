Emergency Dental FAQ

What is considered a dental emergency in Sarasota? +
A dental emergency includes any situation involving uncontrolled bleeding, severe pain that prevents sleep, swelling that affects breathing, or a knocked-out permanent tooth.
Do you accept walk-in patients? +
Yes, our Sarasota network is specifically designed to accommodate same-day walk-in patients who require immediate triage and pain relief.
